Common questions

How do I add an item to my wardrobe?

Tap Capture at the bottom of the screen, point your camera at the item, and shoot. Kaveo removes the background, tags the item automatically, and adds it to your wardrobe. You can also import from your photo library or paste a product URL.

How do I delete my account?

Open Settings → Account → Delete account. This permanently removes all your photos, items, outfits, wishlist, and profile data. There is no recovery — make sure to export anything you want to keep first.

I forgot my password — how do I reset it?

On the sign-in screen tap Forgot password? and enter your email. We'll send you a reset link that's valid for one hour.

An item came out wrong — bad tags or background still showing.

Open the item, tap the edit icon, and correct the category, color, or notes manually. If the background wasn't removed, retake the photo against a plain surface with even light — that gives Kaveo the best chance.

Bulk-upload got stuck or some items never finished processing.

Pull-to-refresh on the wardrobe screen, or tap Resume processing if you see that button near the top. If items still don't finish, email us with your account email and we'll investigate.

Do my photos stay private?

Yes. Photos are stored in your private account on Supabase (EU region) and are only visible to you. We never share or sell them. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

Is Kaveo free?

Kaveo is free during the beta. We'll introduce an optional paid tier later for power users — existing wardrobes will not be locked behind it.
